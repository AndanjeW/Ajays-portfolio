French Toast

Ingredient
  • 3 thick slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 spoons of milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup margarine (you can use oil
  • ¼sliced mangoes and yogurt to garnish
Method
  • Step 1
    Mix the egg and vanilla (I added salt but you can add a spoonful of sugar if you have a sweet tooth) in a bowl. Melt a spoonful of the margarine in a frying pan over medium heat.
  • Step 2
    Take one slice of bread and dip it in the egg on one side then the other making sure it is evenly coated on both sides.Let the egg drip off from the side for a few seconds cook the bread slice for about 2 minutes (until it turns a golden browned) and repeat for the remaining slices

Chow Mein (Stir Fried Noodles)

Ingredient
  • 350g spaghetti or linguine
  • 140g pack diced pancetta, or smoked streaky bacon
  • 2 stp olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 egg, plus 4 yolks
  • 50g parmesan, grated
Method
  • Step 1
    In a small mixing bowl, combine ingredients for chow mein sauce: oyster sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce, chicken broth, and cornstarch. Set aside. Cook noodles according to package instructions, set aside
  • Step 2
    Heat a large wok or pan. Add a bit of oil to the pan and cook pork until it’s golden brown. Add veggies and saute for a few minutes until veggies are slightly softened.
  • Step 3
    toss in the cooked noodles and pour the sauce right on top. Cook for about 2 minutes.Remove from the heat and serve hot
